About Me

I’m an amateur designer with a passion for visual communication and clear storytelling. My journey started when I became The chairwoman of the Press Club at our school (Statplume) during the 2023/2024 academic year, where I discovered Canva as a powerful tool to engage audiences. Since then, I’ve been using it for academic projects, presentations, and even student life events on demand.

Today, I combine creativity with my background in Statistics and Economics as an ISE student at ENSAE Pierre Ndiaye, Dakar. I create flyers, decks, and reports in Canva — and when needed, I bring these ideas to life on the web.
Visuals that are clean, stories that connect — that’s my craft.
